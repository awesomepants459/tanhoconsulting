About Our Staff

Tan Ho, Founder and CEO

Tan Ho is the visionary founder and CEO of Tan Ho Travel Consulting, a leading firm specializing in optimizing excursions for major European tour operators. With over 20 years of experience in the global travel industry, Tan has honed his expertise in blending financial acumen with innovative planning strategies to create inclusive and informative travel experiences. Born in Vietnam and raised in the United States, he holds a Master's degree in Business Administration from the University of California, Berkeley, and began his career managing high-end tours for luxury operators across Europe. Tan's passion for sustainable tourism and data-driven decision-making led him to establish the company in 2018, where he oversees strategic partnerships and ensures every client receives tailored solutions that enhance profitability while prioritizing traveler satisfaction.

Kowit Ho, Co-Founder and Managing Director

Kowit Ho, Tan's brother and co-founder of Tan Ho Travel Consulting, serves as the Director of Operations, bringing a hands-on approach to streamlining excursion logistics for European tour operators. With a background in supply chain management and a Bachelor's degree in International Business from Stanford University, Kowit has spent 15 years optimizing operational efficiencies in the hospitality sector, including roles at major firms in Asia and Europe. His expertise in inclusive planning ensures that excursions are accessible to diverse groups, from families to adventure seekers. Kowit's collaborative spirit and attention to detail have been instrumental in scaling the company's services, allowing for seamless integration of financial audits with real-time market insights.

Jeff Spansail, Analyst and Equity Partner

Jeff Spansail is the Lead Analyst at Tan Ho Travel Consulting, where he spearheads data-driven analyses to optimize excursions for top European tour operators. He helps to approach things from a financially-friendly standpoint. He handles the company's accounting work, making sure that not only can we manage money well in travel, but our own business as well.
