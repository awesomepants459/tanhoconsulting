  Sample Excursion

This excursion is just one of many possibilities using our methods described on the home page. We use those methods to save money in a lot of different scenarios.



Day 1 Private transfer from Florence Airport (FLR) to a centrally located 4-star hotel, selected for accessibility (e.g., elevators, wheelchair-friendly rooms).
.
Evening walking tour of Florence’s UNESCO World Heritage historic center, including the Duomo, Ponte Vecchio, and Piazza della Signoria. Tan Ho’s inclusive planning ensures guides provide multilingual commentary and visual aids for hearing-impaired travelers.
.
Welcome dinner featuring Tuscan specialties like ribollita and bistecca alla fiorentina, with vegan and gluten-free options available.
.
Day 2 Guided tour of the Uffizi Gallery and Accademia (home to Michelangelo’s David), with skip-the-line access optimized through Tan Ho’s data-driven scheduling to avoid peak crowds.
.
Afternoon workshop on Tuscan leather crafting, designed for hands-on engagement and accessible to all ages.
.
Free time for shopping at San Lorenzo Market, with recommendations from Tan Ho’s analytics on top-rated local vendors.
.
Day 3 Morning transfer to Siena (1.5 hours by air-conditioned coach with Wi-Fi and charging ports).
.
Guided tour of Siena’s Gothic Cathedral and Piazza del Campo, home of the Palio horse race. Tan Ho’s inclusive design includes audio guides and tactile maps for visually impaired guests.
.
Evening at a family-run trattoria, with a menu optimized for dietary inclusivity based on customer feedback data.
.
Day 4 Day trip to the medieval hilltop town of San Gimignano, known for its 14 towers and UNESCO status.
.
Afternoon wine-tasting tour in the Chianti region, visiting sustainable vineyards vetted by Tan Ho’s financial audits for cost-efficiency and eco-friendly practices.
.
Accessible transport options (e.g., low-entry vehicles) ensure all travelers can participate comfortably.
.
Day 5 Private transfer to Florence Airport, with Tan Ho’s optimized scheduling ensuring timely arrivals based on flight data analysis.
.
Farewell gift: A locally sourced keepsake (e.g., Tuscan pottery), selected for sustainability and cultural authenticity.
.
